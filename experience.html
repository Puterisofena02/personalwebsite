    • MY EXPERIENCE

    My experience during the last semester holiday, I and my friend went on vacation to Langkawi Island for 3 days and 2 nights. This experience is very interesting because this is my first vacation with my friend. On the island of Langkawi, my friend and I went to some interesting places. Dayang Bunting Lake, Beras Basah Island, and Cenang Beach are some of them. These three places are very interesting, and most tourists who come to the island of Langkawi go there.
